About Us

Engineering the Future of Africa's Energy

KATCHCON is a privately owned organization incorporated in the Republic of Kenya in August 2012. We specialize in the provision of engineering consulting services within the sub-Saharan Africa region, delivering excellence across power generation, transmission, distribution, and energy storage sectors.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, innovative engineering consultancy services that drive sustainable power and energy development across sub-Saharan Africa. We are committed to delivering solutions that meet international standards while addressing local needs.

Our Vision

To be a leader in the provision of high-quality engineering solutions, recognized for our technical expertise, innovation, and commitment to powering Africa's sustainable development through clean and efficient energy infrastructure.

Our Team

Expertise That Delivers

Assembled and staffed by a complementary team of Advisors, Partners, and Technical Specialists, KATCHCON is equipped to deliver on a diverse range of power and energy projects across a broad spectrum of functional areas.

Our experts are involved in upstream development of power and energy projects through implementation, including testing and commissioning. We maintain solid partnerships with local and international firms who complement our experience.
